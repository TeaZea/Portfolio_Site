welcome

My name is Stefano, an aspiring Software Developer with CMS and marketing experience. I've been self-teaching full time, with the help of CS50, other online resources and a small network of professional developers to help guide me on my journey. As of right now, I have a preference for frontend development. I'm dedicated to writing clear and beautiful code and adapt quickly to situations. I'm a huge tech nerd and strive to find innovative and creative solutions to challenges.

some stuff and things about me

I am a proud Canadian with Italian heritage, currently living in Toronto, Ontario with my very okay wife and our two cats. Not only am I a huge tech nerd, but also a pretty big gamer (who would have guessed). When I’m not defending Azeroth, I enjoy the company of friends and family, watching the Toronto Maple Leafs lose and just generally going with the flow.

Before my wife encouraged me to study software development full time, I was a commercial and imaging producer in radio for the last 10 years. My job took me all over Ontario - from Timmins, to Sarnia to Barrie. It was a ton of fun and I met a lot of amazing people, some of whom I still keep in contact with today.

Becoming a developer was something I should have done A LOT sooner. I love the challenges of backend development, the inspiring creativity of frontend development and the immense satisfaction of tackling challenges together in a team.
